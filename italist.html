Context

The purpose of this experience design intervention was to find an experience design opportunity and propose an implementable solution that bridges business and design. Ultimately, the final form of the intervention was a website redesign that brings forth the Italian boutique product suppliers that are present in Italist's business model.

About Italist

Italist is an online luxury fashion marketplace based in Los Angeles that provides a unique value to customers by partnering with Italian boutiques which allows them to offer a more unique selection of brands and products.

Brand Value Pillars

After researching Italist's brand and reading how customers perceive the company, the team extracted three brand pillars that guided the design intervention:

  1. Small Boutique Partners: Italist partners with small, Italian boutiques to provide authentic and prsitine luxury goods.
  2. Luxury Discovery: Global customers have access to over 1,500 international luxury brands carefully curated by the Italist team and their partner boutiques.
  3. Shop Italian from America: Italist provides greater international reach for their partnered boutiques, allowing international customers to feel as if they are shopping in Italy.
