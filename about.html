More about me.

I'm a designer with a focus on branding, UI/UX, and visual storytelling, always aiming to bring clarity, connection, and meaning to the work.

I studied Interactive Arts & Technology at SFU, with a design concentration, and have experience helping brands tell their stories through thoughtful design rooted in strategy and empathy. I thrive in teams through strong collaboration, the ability to pivot on ideas, and draw connections across design, marketing, and business.

Where design meets strategy

I thrive where design and communication meet, turning insights into visuals that solve real problems. My work blends brand strategy, storytelling, and design systems that adapt across platforms. I care about details, but always design with the bigger picture in mind: how a brand feels, why it matters, and how it connects with people.
